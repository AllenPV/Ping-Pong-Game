AI PingPong Game





Instructions

1.First keep your laptop straight

2.Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop

3.Move your right wrist in ascending and descending manner,a red dot should appear on your right wrist

4.Now press the play button,and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop

5.The red paddle is your paddle,that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
